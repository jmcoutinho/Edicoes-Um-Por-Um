1:1 is a self-publishing library on Instagram.

Working with a variety of artists from a web-fuelled global scene, we’re aiming at dwelling on the boundaries of Instagram’s frameworks to explore how short form narrative can be generated through subverting the platform’s restrictions.

In early 2017 Instagram issued a new feature, allowing its users to upload up to 10 files in one post.
Soon after, visual artists using the platform were exploring the new possibilities this opportunity presented and it wasn’t long before the first Instagram-based magazines started surfacing.
Inspired by these sub-cultural movements and interested in their parallels with fanzine culture, Francisco and João decided to create Edições Um Por Um, hoping it becomes a place where experimentation is nurtured and ideas are shared and iterated.
